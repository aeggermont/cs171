Unemployment: US vs. China

This line chart compares the unemployment rate between the US and China. Overall, it's interesting to note that the two countries converge towards similar unemployment rates from very different positions.

The data is from the International Labour Organization (ILO). Note that the most recent data available is from 2005 and China only started compiling data in 1985 (while the US began in 1981). In addition, comments were manually added to interesting and relevant datapoints.
